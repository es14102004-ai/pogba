ZIZO

Ahmed Mostafa Mohamed Sayed, known as Zizou (born January 10, 1996), is an Egyptian footballer who plays for Zamalek Club in the Egyptian Premier League and the Egypt national football team as a winger. His football career Zizo is a graduate of the Cairo branch of JMG Academy. In December 2012, he moved to the youth team of Wadi Degla Club, and in the summer of 2013 he signed a contract with the Belgian club Lierse. On 29 March 2014, in the match against Waasland-Beveren, he made his debut in the Belgian League. On October 4, against Zulte-Waregem, Zizou scored his first goal for Lierse. In total, Zizou played 77 matches for the Belgian club, scoring 5 goals and providing 10 assists.

In early 2017, Zizou moved on loan until the end of the 2017-18 season to Club Nacional. On January 28, in a match against Arauca, he made his debut in the Portuguese League. On 31 March, in a match against Vitoria de Guimarães, Zizou scored his first goal for Nacional. In the summer of 2017, Zizou moved to Moreirense. On September 10, in the match against Estoril Praia, he made his debut for the new club. In the same match, Zizou scored his only goal for Moreirense. Zizou failed to become an important player for the team, scoring only two goals in 31 matches for Moreirense. In April 2018, he was unable to obtain a Portuguese work visa, due to which he was no longer able to play any competitive matches.
